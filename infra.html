Infrastructure

The college is housed in a 4-acre campus with spacious, well-furnished classrooms and well-equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology, Microbiology, Genetics, Nutrition, Bio-Technology, Psychology, and a Museum. A modern state-of-the-art computer center is located in a fully air-conditioned block. There is a acoustically treated auditorium, spacious audio-visual rooms, well-furnished conference hall, and a separate block to accommodate the P.G. Courses. Language teaching is aided by the well-equipped language labs for English and French. Smart boards and LCDs add to the learning experience at St. Ann’s. The digital display boards installed in the corridors show the latest updates of the events in the campus.
